Our Story

The NOR Philosophy

Born from a desire to bridge the gap between fine perfumery and automotive luxury, NOR was founded on the principle that your journey should be as exquisitely crafted as your destination.

We refuse to compromise on quality. While traditional car air fresheners rely on synthetic gels and overpowering artificial scents, NOR utilizes 100% natural essential oils, ethically sourced from around the globe, delivered through our proprietary aerospace-grade aluminum and ceramic core diffusers.

The Composition

A harmonious blend of rare botanicals and pure essential oils. Our proprietary extraction process ensures the molecules retain their peak potency, delivering a scent that evolves as you drive.

How to Use

Gently slide the NOR diffuser onto your vehicle's air vent. The airflow will naturally activate the ceramic core, releasing the fragrance. Adjust the air intensity to control the diffusion speed.
